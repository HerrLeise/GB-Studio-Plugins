### Select
With the `select` type you can define a list of options to choose from, in the event definition UI.

```
  .
  .
  .
  	type: "select",
	options: [
		["right", "Right"],
		["left", "Left"]
	],
	defaultValue: "right",
    .
    .
    .
```
